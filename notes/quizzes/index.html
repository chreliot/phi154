Quizzes

(Sep 28)

We will be having regular check-up quizzes. Quizzes will be very short—normally 10 minutes. Their purpose is to check in on your understanding and to incentivize your ongoing efforts, should they require a hint of additional incentive.

  • Some quizzes will be open notes and homework, meaning that you can copy answers from your own homework (though not from anyone else). In those cases, the quizzes will exactly repeat some homework problems.

  • Some quizzes will be closed notes and homework, meaning that you’ll just use your pen/pencil and what’s in your mind. In those cases, the quizzes will closely resemble (but may not be identical to) your homework tasks.

Let me know if you have any questions. If the question is broadly applicable, I might post it, without your name, but with my answer, to the course website under “Notes,” unless you ask me not to.
